Call For Papers

“

Department of Civil Engineering, Walchand College of Engineering, Sangli (An Autonomous Institute) is organizing an international conference on "Recent advances in Civil and Environmental Engineering" on 4th and 5th March 2017.
The original and unpublished research/review papers are invited for presentation in the conference. The papers received will undergo a review process from experts in the relevant areas and a maximum of 40 papers will be selected for the conference. Of these accepted papers 30 papers will be selected for oral presentation and the rest will be permitted for poster presentation. The selected papers will be published in proceedings with ISBN number.
The template for preparing the paper is provided on the website www.walchandsangli.ac.in/RACEE2017
Camera ready papers may please be submitted on icracee17@walchandsangli.ac.in

The themes of conference include :

Track 1 – Civil Engineering

  • Construction and Structural Engineering
  • Geo-Technical Engineering
  • Green Technologies in Civil Engineering
  • Applications of Soft Computing, Remote Sensing, GIS and GPS in Civil Engineering
  • Transportation Engineering

Track 2 – Environmental Engineering

  • Water Conservation and Recycling
  • Solid Waste Management
  • Air Pollution and Air Quality Modeling
