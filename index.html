The conference is going to be held in Civil Engineering Department of Walchand College of Engineering, Sangli (MS) on 4th and 5th March 2017. The RACEE - 2017 aims at presenting recent advances in the research areas of Civil and Environmental Engineering. It is going to be wonderful platform for the delegates to exchange their experiences with world renowned plenary and featured speakers.

About College

Established in the year 1947, Walchand College of Engineering (WCE) is one of the reputed colleges in India. Affiliated to Shivaji University, Kolhapur, this college was granted autonomy in the year 2007 and has been one of the few selected colleges in India undergoing the MHRD funded projects under TEQIP. The college has been accredited by NAAC with “A” grade and most of the academic programmes are accredited by NBA.

WCE is located on Belagavi-Pune railway route and the convenient airports are Belagavi (120 kms), Pune (230 Kms) and Mumbai (450 kms) from Sangli.

About Department

The Department of Civil Engineering was the first inchoative discipline at Walchand College of Engineering, Sangli and the oldest in Southern Maharashtra region. The Department offers Bachelor’s Degree in Civil Engineering (B.Tech.) and two programmes leading to Master’s degree (M.Tech.) in Civil engineering with specialization of Environmental Engineering and Structural Engineering adequately supported with doctoral programmes in Civil Engineering. The department has been accredited for five years by NBA consistently twice since 2003.

The Department is known for its reputed teachers having expertise in diverse fields. The department continues to strive loftier by exploring new frontiers of civil engineering, imparting the latest technical knowledge to the students and conducting high quality research which renders technical advice on live engineering problems in the field of Environmental Engineering to various Government and Private Sector organizations throughout the state. It has built up a wide research potential in Environmental Engineering, Geotechnical Engineering and Building Technologies. The research projects of the department are funded by various agencies such as Ministry of Human Resource Development (MHRD), All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), Maharashtra Pollution Control Board (MPCB), Department of Science and Technology (DST) etc.





VMO's

VISION

To be a Civil Engineering programme of the first choice by the aspiring students and prospective employers by implementing world class education practices.




MISSION

To meet the Vision, all stakeholders of this programme are committed towards Outcome Based Education Philosophy by


1. Adopting vibrant academic curricula and implementing innovative teaching learning processes.

2. Providing opportunities to the students for the development of professional skills.

3. Nurturing critical thinking and creativity in students.

4. Inculcating in students the life-long learning attitude and sensitivity towards society & environment.



OBJECTIVES


1. To achieve high level of technical expertise to understand, identify, formulate, design and implement a real life Civil Engineering problem.

2. To inculcate the leadership and ethical qualities in implementing the projects.

3. To produce technically competent and ethically sound engineering graduates.

4. To satisfy the requirement of society through active participation in infrastructural development.

5. To prepare the students towards competitive examinations, research and higher education.

6. To induce qualities of life-long learning, working in teams with effective communication skills and interaction with society.
