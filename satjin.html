Main relevant assignments and projects:

  • April 2011 – April 2016

As advisor there was also the task to establish links between Malinese representatives of the national water sector, universities and knowledge institutes and the Dutch water sector. Since 2013 responsible for the crosscutting themes water and climate and water and food security, supporting the country specific strategic advisors and the embassies of the focus countries of MFA, including Mali.

    • Chairman of the commission (part time) for the Mid Term Evaluation of Partners for Water Program 3.

    • Program coordinator (part time) of the Coordinated Program on Water and Climate, knowledge brokering and networking with the members of the Netherlands Water Partnership, the Dutch water sector and the international development aid network, including the WWF network, the WB, GWP, IFAD, etc. One subject of CPWC was the development of the Framework for Sustainable Development to support the international IWRM work.

After July 2011 as senior advisor / associate for 1.5 days per week active in the booster team with an emphasis on international projects, among others as with Ministry of Foreign Affairs.


  • November 2003-July 2013

    • Director (full time) of the research program Living with Water, with a total budget of € 56 million focusing on the paradigm shift from “defense against the water” to “living with water” by developing, implementing and promoting a program with some 120 projects and 200 consortium partners. The program team had a capacity of totally 8 FTE with three project coordinators and six supporting staff. In addition to the duties as program director there were tasks as project supervising, including the supervision of projects in Gabon, Japan and China.

    • Member of the Board (2006-2010) and Chairman of the project selection committee of the Dutch Waterboard Fund (2010-2014), an organization with a budget of around € 1 million / year focused on supporting water boards in their international work. Special attention was given to support the project for a climate-proof design of the mouth of the Senegal river delta in the city of St. Louis
